About PHP

Pro Home Painters, LLC is a fictional Eastern Connecticut based professional painting company. PHP specializes in exterior and interior home painting but also provides services such as colonial restorations, cabinetry and staining. Pro Home Painters prides themselves in for their unique and challenging projects and 98% customer satisfaction rate.
