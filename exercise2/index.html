What you want to learn in this course

Since I have been developing website both professionally and as a freelancer for many years now I didn't expect to learn anything earth shattering. However, I learned early on that I can learn a lot from any course and any group of people. This all said I am hopeful to revisit the basics along with some unknown tooling, tips and tricks. Often the past gets lost or forgotten when in reality it seems to just repeat itself in new "cool" tricks. I will close out that this course so far has reminded me of CSS Naked Day, a day which should be remembered when creating websites!
