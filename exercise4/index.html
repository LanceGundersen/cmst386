Accessibility

Tab Order

Tab order is the order in which the users will navigate a website via the TAB key. There are a few simple rules regarding the implementation of tab order which can be reviewed here in greater detail. Tab order is important to allow for non-keyboard and mouse navigation of a website for good accessibility by both disabled and non-disabled persons.

WCAG2/WAI

WCAG2 is the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines which defines how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological disabilities. WAI is the Web Accessibility Initiative by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) which is an effort to improve the accessibility of the web for people with disabilities. WAI is commited to increasing awareness, guidance for organizations leadership and provide policies and procedures.

Color Contrast

Color contrast analyzers help visually impaired users to see the website pages. This also is true for non-visually impaired users in poor lighting conditions such as direct sunlight on a phone screen. Many frameworks provide accessibility and color contrasting features built in.

Usability

User-centered Design

User centered design is a design process that keeps the user focused in each part of the design. The users are involved in throughout the design process and research phases to help create a highly approachable end product. This process generally is iterative in nature and has stakeholders and users conduct A/B testing of designs along with provide detailed feedback in designs and user flows. These usability tests are often task oriented.

Usability

Usability is simply an assessment of how easy to use an interface is to use. It can also refer to the designs attributes on ease-of-use such as learnability, efficiency, memorability, errors, and satisfaction.

Brand Awareness

Increased brand awareness adds credibility, authority and trust to the content and brand overall. Users will tend to share social media articles, images and videos from brands they know and trust which adds to natural social media marketing and continued brand awareness efforts.

SEO

Link Signals

Link signals provide a search engine with an idea of how important your website may be to a particular topic or domain. The link signals are simple conceptually in that a link from or to a credible and relevant source provides validation and importance to your website.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is a powerhouse of tools for the entire website team (designer, developer and owner) as it provides analytical tools and over ten different reports to see how users are using the website. Ultimately the Google Analytics suite allows for the business owner to make informed business decisions.

Social Media Optimization

Social Media Optimization is optimizing, through branding and marketing, your social media presence across multiple social media platforms. For some companies and individuals this is tailored for a certain niche market or is used in a broader context across domains. It's important to optimize to ensure a business or individual has a cohesive social media presence to include imagery (logos & colors) material content such as blogs, videos, etc. Most importantly ensure your social media is relevant to your audience.

Webmaster Tools

Webmaster Tools is a service that helps website owners and webmasters evaluate and maintain their website or applications performance surrounding search results. The tools allows owners to submit web pages, view how content is seen by search engines and discover issues.

Google Analytics

Reports

Google Analytics offers over ten reporting tools that have unique ways of pivoting on the data captured of a sites users. Google even offers real time reporting so you can watch in real time the behaviors and actions of users after a post, video feed, free download offer, etc.

The report information can help identify what content users find useful or not useful. This reporting in conjunction with the behavioral patterns can help bring items forward or optimize the navigation to enhance the users needs.
